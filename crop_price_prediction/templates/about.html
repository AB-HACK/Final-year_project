About PredictAgro

PredictAgro is a machine learning-based crop price prediction system built for smallholder farmers in Nigeria as a BSc final year project.

The goal is to support data-driven decisions by forecasting likely commodity prices ahead of time.

Dataset Information

The project uses the WFP Nigeria food prices dataset (2002-2023), covering market-level commodity price observations across Nigerian states and markets.
